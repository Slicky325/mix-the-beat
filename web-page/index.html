About the project

Mix-the-Beat is a tool to process an audio clip or a song and change its style. It gives a different tone to the input using the technique of "music style transfer". It aims to allow more creative freedom to artists and open up for new possibilities allowing music producers to change and explore the general feel of the music. Using this tool, Provide a music clip (The content input), there will be two options to process you music, the editor and the merger.
